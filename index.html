Ty the Garden Guy

Hi, I'm Ty and I'm passionate about helping people design and maintain beautiful, sustainable gardens. I offer a range of products and services, including permaculture design consultations, gardening workshops, and online courses. Take a look at my offerings and let me know how I can help you bring your gardening dreams to life.

Permaculture Consultation

Need help designing a sustainable and productive garden? I offer permaculture design consultations to help you create a beautiful and functional space that works with nature. Whether you have a small backyard or a large farm, I can help you create a design that meets your needs and goals.

Gardening Workshop

Join me for a hands-on gardening workshop where you'll learn the skills and knowledge you need to successfully grow your own food. From soil preparation to plant selection to pest control, I'll cover everything you need to know to create a thriving garden. Workshops are offered in-person and online, so you can choose the option that works best for you.

Online Course

Can't make it to a workshop in person? No problem! I offer a range of online courses that cover everything from permaculture principles to garden design to plant care. These courses are self-paced, so you can learn at your own pace and on your own schedule. Sign up today and start your journey to becoming a successful gardener.
